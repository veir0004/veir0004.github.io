About Me

I’m Nichole Veiro and I live in Ottawa, Ontario. Currently continuing to learn and gain experience about Graphic Design. I am in my third year of the Graphic Design program at Algonquin College. I was always interested in Art and Design from a young age. When it came time to choosing a career path I decided to go to school to gain knowledge and the skills it takes to make it in the industry and work on building an amazing portfolio.

I still have a lot to learn but it is worth it if it means I will spend my life doing something I enjoy and I can contribute my style to the world of graphic design. I don’t have any specific specialties at the moment but I have some experience in a lot of areas such as Print Design, Branding, Photography, Motion Graphics and UI&UX Design.

NIKE AD ILLUSTRATION

Print Illustration

This is an Ad by Nike that I created with the slogan I Wont Lose. I chose this slogan because athletes usually scream or express emotion after they score during games. To create this piece I used an image as reference. The image is of Kevin after making a shot during a game. His facial expression matches the slogan. I free handedly drew triangles over the image in Adobe Illustrator until I finished his entire face.

You can view the entire project on Behance.

  • Date: Sept 2014

ORIEVO BEER

Package/Branding Design

Orievo is a clean and minimal concept of a beer brand I came up with. I noticed that most beers have the same look. I wanted to stay away from the traditional look and try a modern style. The name Orievo is my last name backwards with an extra O. Orievo comes in four different styles which are Blonde, Lime, Red and Strong.

You can view the entire project on Behance.

ORLEANS FRESH FRUIT

Branding Design

Orleans Fresh Fruit is an existing company in Ottawa, Ontario that supplies fruit and vegetable products to places like restaurants, hotels, hospitals and nursing homes throughout Eastern Ontario and Western Quebec. I took a clean and modern style to the logo to keep the brand looking fresh and approachable to viewers.

THE SOAP WORKS

Branding Design

This is a rebrand concept for a company based in Toronto, Ontario. The products are glycerine based soaps that use old-fashioned methods and natural ingredients in order to solve any kind of skin problems people get from popular mainstream soaps. This company doesn’t use any packaging or branding at all. I decided to design this new packaging which is eco-friendly as well stylish. Consumers can now match the product with the brand.

You can view the entire project on Behance.

FONTIFY APP DESIGN

UI/UX Design

Fontify is an app for iOS that helps users identify what font is being used anywhere at anytime. You simply take a picture of the font and the app will let you know what font is being used and also giving suggestions of similar fonts. With this app you will never have to wonder or miss out on that awesome font again.

You can view the entire project on Behance.
